Student Ambassador

Are you a highly driven, social media savvy student (any discipline) or recent graduate (within the past 2 years) interested in mental health, start-ups and being at the cutting edge of health tech? If so, we would like you to join our talented team of student ambassadors that will be helping to shape our product and make a real difference in young people’s lives.

Role description

  • Reaching out to your student networks to recruit students for in-depth interviews and focus groups (networking & recruiting)
  • Represent beok and our mission in public events, conference and 1-2-1 meetings with stakeholders and universities (comms & outreach)
  • Whenever possible, conduct interviews and facilitate group discussions in relation to beok’s discovery phase stage (research & dev)

Benefits

  • Access to our network for career advice and support
  • Develop and improve your communication, team working and leadership skills
  • Great extracurricular activity to boost your career prospects and CV
  • Opportunities for further progression as part of beok’s core team (subject to your area of interest and ability to contribute in an early-stage start-up)

Mental Health Professionals (Psych, Clinical Psych, Counsellors, Psychotherapists) interested in Advisory Roles

We are looking to recruit clinical advisors and mental health professionals specialising in working with young people (and students in particular) in the context of mental health support services. Join our advisory board and help us design a cutting-edge tech solution to support and promote young people’s mental wellbeing.

If you are interested in any of these opportunities, please contact us (including your CV or LinkedIn profile link) at hello@beok.io
