mission

beok is an early stage start-up that aims to develop and build a digital mental wellbeing platform for university students focusing on innovative tech solutions and evidence-based research.

get involved

We are interested in speaking to students (any discipline, any level) and recent graduates and book you in for our in-depth, anonymous interviews. You can help us understand what kind of services work best and design the solution.
We want to hear from you!

vision

We believe in the future where no young people are left struggling with mental health issues alone. Improving access to mental health support services for students.
