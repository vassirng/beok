OUR VISION

We believe in the future where no young people are left struggling with mental health issues alone. Improving access to psychological therapy for students. Anytime. Anywhere.

OUR MISSION

We aim to develop a digital mental wellbeing platform to provide timely, continuous and personalised psychological support to students in the UK using innovative tech solutions and evidence-based research.
